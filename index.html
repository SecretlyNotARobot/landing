Who I am
Born and raised in New Hamburg, ON, I am a full stack developer with experience in robotics, cloud, edge development and everything in between. My introduction to programming was a trial by fire, working with openCV on a headless Raspberry Pi. Undaunted, I moved on to modern apps working with static web development, games and early NodeJS servers.
Recently, I have been learning the ropes as a three-time co-op at PEER Group. At the end of the day, I feel confident in my abilities in C#, C++, Agile, JS, Java, Git, Azure and SOLIDWORKS
